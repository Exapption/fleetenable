About Fleet Enable

Founded in 2019, Fleet Enable was built by the experienced team of transportation
technology experts at Imaginnovate. Our software solutions have been put to the test by the biggest
names in the industry, and now we’re ready to #DeliverBetter to the Final Mile leaders of tomorrow.
Today we’re working with a select set of carriers who are ready to upgrade to a better Final
Mile experience that fosters growth, empowers teams and drivers and quickly scales.
Request a demo to see how Fleet Enable can deliver for you.

Why Choose Us

Fleet Enable can help you #DeliverBetter. Consumer expectations are higher than ever, but so is the demand for home delivery. With our automated solution, you can lower operational costs, eliminate repetitive tasks and scale your business.

Our Mission

Fleet Enable’s mission is to automate white glove services and maximize profit for carriers. Our end-to-end Final Mile Management System makes enterprise-level technology accessible for Carriers of any size.

What We Do

Fleet Enable is a cloud-based cutting-edge technology solution that is configurable for all Carriers. From order and exception management to the driver mobile experience, Fleet Enable puts automated Final Mile routing, dispatch, billing, invoicing, driver pay and customer management technology within reach.
