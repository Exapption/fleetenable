Features
Route optimization
FleetEnable route optimizer considers order delivery time, locations and fleet capacity to provide the most optimized routes.
Auto-dispatch
Routes can be assigned to agents automatically in multiple ways. They can also enforce the agents to pick any route if it is not grabbed by any driver.
Real-time data
Fleet enables provide live updates of the routes and orders to operations team, customers and drivers. Operations team can communicate to drivers and vice versa in realtime.
Proof of delivery
Fleetenable enables the drivers to capture barcodes, pictures and customer signatures. Under the hoods the location and timestamps are also captured.
Efficient Driver Operations through easy navigation
Mobile apps help drivers by providing step by step route information and also can leverage any of the mapping apps available on his mobile like google maps, ways etc.
Feedback collection
Feedback can be collected from the app or website for an order. This can be integrated to analytics to improve the overall customer experience.
Automatic status updates via notifications and alerts
Drivers and customers are notified by SMS, Email and Push notifications. Customers can know the status of delivery and can track their order.
Customizable and flexible
Your logo, your colors and app features customized to your business and customer needs.
Enterprise ready
Platform is thoroughly tested and proven. It is can be easily integrated and scalable to cater your needs.
Mobile Apps
Mobile Apps for iOS and Android with best in class experience.
360 Degrees visibility
With realtime data and communication to different endpoints to customers, drivers and operations provides a clear visibility of the business operations.
Analytics
FleetEnable captures analytics as required by the client. These provide the performance metrics of Last Mile Delivery and help in improving the customer experience and operational efficiency.
